Privacy PolicyGizlilik Politikası

Last updated: May 2025


Akanzi ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected when you use Akanzi and how it is used.


1. DATA WE COLLECT


Location
We request access to your device's location while the app is in use. This is used solely to search for nearby places via the Google Places API. We do not store your location on any server — all processing happens on your device.


Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
With your permission (via the App Tracking Transparency prompt), AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier to deliver personalised ads. If you decline, non-personalised ads will be shown instead.


App Data
Groups, members, and saved spots are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.


2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES


  • Google Places API — Used to search and display nearby places. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — Used to display ads. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. DATA SHARING
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above via Google services.


4. DATA RETENTION
All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data. We retain no data on external servers.


5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Akanzi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.


6. CONTACT
Questions? Reach us at: gibbonook@gmail.com
